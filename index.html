About the School

Serving the community with purpose and pride

Pakistan School of Excellence stands as a center of academic progress and moral development in Pirkhel, District Malkand. We strive to provide a safe, encouraging, and disciplined environment where students can grow academically, socially, and spiritually.

Our school believes in a balanced approach to education where strong teaching, student support, and values-based learning work together to create confident and responsible young leaders.
